URL Shortener Microservice

This app shortens URLs.

Just type in /new/[URL YOU WANT TO SHORTEN] at the end of this page's URL, and you will get a shortened version of the original URL.

Example

Input: 'https://url-shortener-sebgrebe.herokuapp.com/new/https://facebook.com'

Output: {"original_url":'https://facebook.com',"shortened_url":"https://url-shortener-sebgrebe.herokuapp.com/0"}
